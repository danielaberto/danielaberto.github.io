Hello! I'm Daniela.

I research, write and produce animated films,
visual essays, and interactive formats that helps to understand subjects in a clear and concise way.

I experiment through different context, media and technologies, in collaboration with writers, journalists, directors, developers, animators, designers, and researchers of any type.

I'm currently starting a new project (text/essay + animation + interactive framework) to explain how the implementation of new technologies will help spreading the Open Access/Open Science mode of publishing.

In 2013 I've started a side project called 'VisualBook', which has become an in-depth research on arguments/ language/debate analysis. The aim is to achieve a better understanding and synthesis about critical themes, taking into account multiple perspectives.
