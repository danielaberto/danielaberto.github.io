Hello! I'm Daniela

I research, write and produce short animated videos, visual essays and multimodal formats that help to convey the essence of a subject in a clear and accessible way.

I run an independent studio - Neue Big - experimenting with different media, context and technologies, in collaboration with writers, directors, animators, developers, designers, and researchers of any type.

Here’s a selection of commercial, cultural and academic work work created since 2007.

I hold a Master’s Degree in Law (my Thesis, in Philosophy of Law/ Bioethic, investigated an Italian case of Euthanasia and analyzed the international debate across disciplines and perspectives) and a post degree Master in Digital Economy.

Over the last 4 years I had to deal with 5 surgeries and health issues, that slowed my life and work down, but this helped to reconnected with my passions and purpose.
I’m currently interested and researching the field of health/medical humanities.
