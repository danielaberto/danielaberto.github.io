Hello!

I'm Daniela, Creative Producer based in Berlin

I research, write and facilitate the creation of communication frameworks and media forms, such as animated films, documentaries, visual essays, and interactive forms, that help to convey an idea, a difficult subject, or theme in a clear and understable way.

.

I experiment through different context, media and technologies, in collaboration with writers, journalists, directors, developers, animators, designers, and researchers of any type.

In my past life I was a founder of a digital/design studio and I have done projects for commercial, cultural or no-profit organizations Neue Big/works, previously worked inside an NGO (ActionAid), a 2000 dot.com startup (Lastminute.com) and various companies. I’m now seeking for a new model to create independent, qualitative, sustainable, critical work, free from corporate mindset and unbalanced interests.

Projects

I'm currently working at a new project (text/essay + animation + interactive framework) to explain how the implementation of new technologies will help spreading the Open Access mode of publishing within a European Open Science Infrastructure.
I hold a Master’s Degree in Law (my Thesis, in Philosophy of Law/ Bioethics, investigated an Italian case of Euthanasia and analyzed the international debate from different perspectives: philosophical, legal, ethical, and medical) and a post degree master in Digital Economy, Among my current interests: Medical Humanities, Critical Visual Studies and Critical Tech.

Experiments

In 2013 I've started a side project called 'VisualBook', which has become an in-depth research on arguments/ language/debate analysis. The aim is to achieve a better understanding and synthesis about critical themes, exploring multiple perspectives.
